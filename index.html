Christelations

An Immersive Journey Through Faith and Art

Christelations is born from a singular artistic vision: the blending of biblical scripture and expressive art into what Richard Pierro calls Parable Art. These paintings are abstract verticals — each one depicting a well-known story or moment from the Old Testament, the Torah, or the New Testament — rendered in vivid color, texture, and emotion.

The Experience

A Stroll Through
the Bible

Over more than forty years of devotion to his craft and his faith, Richard Pierro has journeyed through the ancient landscapes of the Holy Land, walked the streets of Jerusalem, and stood at the very sites where scripture came to life. That lived experience — layered with decades of spiritual reflection — is what breathes soul into every brushstroke.

A Stroll Through the Bible is the culmination of that journey: a collection of twenty original paintings, each paired with its scripture reference, installed in Rick's home gallery in Swampscott, Massachusetts. The sentiment of Christelations is woven through every work — a sacred harmony of faith, art, and the universal human longing to encounter the Word not just as text, but as something you can see, feel, and carry with you.

This is not a museum. It is an invitation — to walk through the Word and be moved by it.

Alongside his paintings, Rick has assembled A Stroll Through the Bible — a visual companion that brings together the full collection of Parable Art with its accompanying scripture. This book is an extension of the gallery itself: a way to carry the experience home.
