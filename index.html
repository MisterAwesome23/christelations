The Experience

A Stroll Through
the Bible

Step inside an extraordinary home gallery in Swampscott, Massachusetts — a sacred space where 22 original paintings by artist Richard Pierro transform biblical parables and scripture into vivid, expressive art.

Each painting is paired with its scripture reference, creating a meditative walk through the Word. Rick has spent years crafting works that speak to the heart — abstract and alive, yet deeply contemplative.

This is not a museum. It is an invitation to experience faith through beauty — and to carry something of it with you when you leave.

Bathing in the

Alongside his paintings, Rick has written Bathing in the Warmth of God — a deeply personal meditation on faith, scripture, and the spiritual life that inspired the entire collection.

This companion text illuminates the heart behind the brushstrokes. Each reflection deepens the experience of the gallery, making it the perfect companion to your stroll through the Word.
